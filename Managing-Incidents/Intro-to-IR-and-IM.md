# Introduction to Incident Response (IR) and Incident Management (IM)

## Date
24 February 2026

---

## What is Incident Response (IR)?

Incident Response is the structured approach to detecting, analyzing, containing, and recovering from security incidents.

### Key Phases of IR:
1. Preparation
2. Identification
3. Containment
4. Eradication
5. Recovery
6. Lessons Learned

---

## What is Incident Management (IM)?

Incident Management focuses on the overall handling and coordination of incidents to minimize business impact.

It includes:
- Communication
- Documentation
- Stakeholder coordination
- Reporting

---

## Difference Between IR and IM

| Incident Response | Incident Management |
|-------------------|--------------------|
| Technical handling | Organizational coordination |
| Focus on threat removal | Focus on business continuity |
| Security team driven | Cross-team collaboration |

---

## Key Learning Today

- IR is technical execution.
- IM is process coordination.
- Documentation is critical in both.
